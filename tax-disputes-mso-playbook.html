Key insight: 25/75 lead-to-support split

5. Licensing & ownership

Unlike law firms (Rule 5.4) or medical practices (CPOM), there is no "corporate practice of tax resolution" doctrine. You need zero professional licenses to own a tax resolution firm.

What owners need
Nothing. No CPA, no EA, no law license. Form a standard LLC or corporation. Hire EAs/CPAs as employees. They maintain their own credentials. You handle everything else — marketing, intake, case management, billing, HR.
What employees need
EAs: Federal enrollment, 72hrs CE/3yrs, PTIN
CPAs: State license (not needed for non-attest work but gives credibility)
Tax assistants: PTIN only (if preparing returns)
Attorneys: Only needed for Tax Court — <2% of cases
Fee-sharing advantage
No Rule 5.4 equivalent for CPAs/EAs. Circular 230 has no prohibition on fee-sharing with management entities. Your MSO can structure economics as percentage of revenue, per-case fee, or flat management fee — far more flexibility than legal MSOs.
If you brand as a "CPA firm"
State Board of Accountancy rules apply. Most states allow up to 49% non-CPA ownership. But for tax resolution specifically, there's no commercial advantage to CPA branding. The large national firms (Optima, Anthem, Community Tax) are all standard LLCs.

6. Advertising landscape

Three-layer funnel: Meta creates demand (top), Google PPC captures intent (mid), Google LSA closes with trust (bottom). The national firms are strong on Meta + PPC but structurally weak on LSA.

Google LSA
$30–$80
Cost per lead. Two categories: "Tax Specialist" and "Lawyer." Nationals have landing pages but no real local presence. Your MSO advantage: genuine local offices + reviews.
Google PPC
$15–$50
Cost per click on tax resolution keywords. "Tax relief" = $15–$40. "Tax attorney near me" = $20–$50. Nationals bid aggressively. Target long-tail local keywords.
Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
$25–$60
Cost per lead. Colder leads but larger volume. Interrupt-based. National firms spend heavily here. Your advantage: authenticity + practitioner-to-camera creative.

LSA service toggles to activate

Turn on: IRS audit representation · IRS problem consulting · Tax problem consulting
Leave off: Tax preparation (floods intake with $300 prep leads). IRS filing only if your team can quickly qualify unfiled-return callers as potential collections cases.

"What makes us different than all of the other tax resolution firms is the fact that we deal with audits. 90% of those companies will not do that." — Collections is the volume play; audits are the premium play. Your MSO should start with collections (EA/CPA-deliverable) and layer in audit capability once the operation is proven.
